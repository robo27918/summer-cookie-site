Pantry Notes

Low-Carb Cookie Recipes

All recipes use almond flour and sugar-free sweeteners for big flavor with fewer carbs.

Chocolate Chip

  • 2 cups almond flour
  • 1/3 cup erythritol
  • 1/4 cup butter, melted
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 cup sugar-free chocolate chips

Bake at 350°F for 10 to 12 minutes.

Oatmeal Raisin

  • 1 1/2 cups almond flour
  • 1/2 cup rolled oats (gluten-free)
  • 1/4 cup erythritol
  • 1/4 cup butter, softened
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/3 cup raisins (reduced)

Bake at 350°F for 11 to 13 minutes.

Sugar Cookie

  • 2 cups almond flour
  • 1/3 cup powdered erythritol
  • 1/4 cup butter, softened
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/4 tsp almond extract
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder

Bake at 325°F for 10 to 12 minutes.

Peanut Butter

  • 1 cup natural peanut butter
  • 1/3 cup erythritol
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • Pinch of salt

Bake at 350°F for 10 to 11 minutes. No flour needed.

Lemon Drop

  • 2 cups almond flour
  • 1/3 cup erythritol
  • 1/4 cup butter, melted
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tbsp fresh lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp lemon zest
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder

Bake at 350°F for 10 to 12 minutes.

Double Chocolate

  • 1 1/2 cups almond flour
  • 1/3 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/3 cup erythritol
  • 1/4 cup butter, melted
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup sugar-free chocolate chips

Bake at 350°F for 11 to 13 minutes.

Red Velvet

  • 2 cups almond flour
  • 2 tbsp cocoa powder
  • 1/3 cup erythritol
  • 1/4 cup butter, melted
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp red food coloring
  • Cream cheese frosting (sugar-free)

Bake at 350°F for 10 to 12 minutes. Top with frosting after cooling.

Fresh From The Oven

Bread Recipes

Classic and low-carb loaves with a playful tab switch so the page still feels light and easy to browse.

Classic White Bread

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 1/4 tsp active dry yeast
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 tsp salt

Rise 1 hour, then bake at 375°F for 25 to 30 minutes.

Whole Wheat Bread

  • 2 cups whole wheat flour
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 1/4 tsp active dry yeast
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 2 tbsp honey
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp salt

Rise 1 hour, then bake at 375°F for 30 to 35 minutes.

Garlic Bread

  • 1 baguette or Italian loaf
  • 1/2 cup butter, softened
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 tbsp fresh parsley
  • Pinch of salt
  • Optional: shredded mozzarella

Bake at 375°F for 10 to 15 minutes until golden.

Banana Bread

  • 3 ripe bananas, mashed
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1/3 cup butter, melted
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Bake at 350°F for 55 to 65 minutes.

Almond Flour Bread

  • 2 cups almond flour
  • 4 eggs
  • 1/4 cup butter, melted
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp apple cider vinegar

Bake at 350°F for 30 to 35 minutes.

Cloud Bread

  • 3 eggs, separated
  • 3 tbsp cream cheese, softened
  • 1/4 tsp cream of tartar
  • Pinch of salt
  • Optional: garlic powder, herbs

Bake at 300°F for 25 to 30 minutes. Light and airy.

Psyllium Husk Bread

  • 1 cup almond flour
  • 1/4 cup psyllium husk powder
  • 3 eggs
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 cup warm water
  • 1/2 tsp salt

Bake at 350°F for 45 to 50 minutes. Great texture.

Low-Carb Banana Bread

  • 2 cups almond flour
  • 1 ripe banana, mashed
  • 3 eggs
  • 1/4 cup erythritol
  • 1/4 cup butter, melted
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Bake at 350°F for 45 to 55 minutes.
